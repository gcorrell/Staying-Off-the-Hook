Key Terms

When discussing any sort of cybersecurity threat, you're likely to run into some technical vocabulary. This section can be used as a reference for the meaning of any unknown terms used on this site.

Deepfake

A digitally altered piece of media (video, audio, etc.) that is made to seem as though it features a paticular person.
